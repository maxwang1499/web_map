NYC Building Emission Map

Check your building's emission and LL97 compliance status.

70% of NYC's greenhouse gas emission comes from the buildings.
In order to reach its 2050 carbon neutrality goal, NYC passed Local Law 97, which places custom carbon thresholds on any building with gross square footage over 25,000 square feet. It charges a penalty for every unit of carbon that exceeds the threshold. The law comes into effect in 2024, with more stringent thresholds coming in 2030.
